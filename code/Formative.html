Discover OPM!

Mabuhay! This page is an encyclopedia of all your favourite OPM artists.

"OPM" stands for "Original Pilipino Music". It's what K-Pop is to Korea, except in the Philippines. OPM emerged around 1970 and has diversified into many different sub-genres. Get an overview of the most popular artists below!
